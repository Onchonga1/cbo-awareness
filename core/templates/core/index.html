Our Mission

To create a society free from gender-based violence and ensure every woman has access to basic sanitary needs.

We conduct awareness programs, provide support to survivors, and distribute sanitary pads to underprivileged women and girls.

What We Do

GBV Awareness

Educational programs and workshops to combat gender-based violence in our communities.

Sanitary Pads Distribution

Providing free sanitary pads to women and girls in need across our community.

Support Groups

Safe spaces for women to share experiences and support each other.
