Our Mission in Africa

To create an African society free from gender-based violence and ensure every woman has access to basic sanitary needs.

We conduct awareness programs across African communities, provide support to survivors, and distribute sanitary pads to underprivileged women and girls in rural and urban areas.

What We Do Across Africa

GBV Awareness in Communities

Educational programs and workshops to combat gender-based violence in African communities and villages.

Sanitary Pads Distribution

Providing free sanitary pads to African women and girls in need across rural and urban communities.

Women Support Groups

Safe spaces for African women to share experiences, support each other, and build resilience.

Our Impact in African Communities

Transforming lives through community engagement and women empowerment.

We work directly with local communities, traditional leaders, and women groups to create sustainable change and break the cycle of gender-based violence.

  • Community outreach programs
  • School-based awareness campaigns
  • Traditional leader engagements
  • Women economic empowerment
